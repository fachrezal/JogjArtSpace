Get in Art and discover the world

Public space is an important element in the urban as a container for interaction and ocialization of urban communities. Yogyakarta as a city of art and culture takes space that can accommodate arts activities but still open to the wider community. Art Gallery is an active form of art space holding various activities for the public. Art Gallery is also a part of the identity of Yogyakarta City as a city of art and culture. Art tourism able to greatly assist in developing economy as it also contributes to other sectors.

One of the most important tools in promoting tourism is map. Tourism mapping is important to provide guidance to tourists in identifying and finding the best tourism spots in the area. However, most tourism maps were not produced using the correct concept and the right tools. The effect of this would lead to lacking of information or misleading to the tourist. This project discusses, based on literatures, the concepts of creating a tourism map through cartography, and introduces the idea of using GIS technology as one of the methods to produce tourism map effectively and efficiently.
